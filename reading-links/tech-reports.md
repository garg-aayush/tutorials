# Technical Reports

- [LFM2 Technical Report](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.23404)
    - Second generation of Liquid Foundation Models (LFMs) that are optimized explicitly for on-device deployment
    - contains: 
        - Hardware-in-the-loop architecture search for on-device efficiency
        - A novel knowledge distillation objective for small model training
        - Three-stage post-training recipes that actually work at small scales
        - `LFM2-VL` for vision-language understanding, `LFM2-Audio` for real-time speech interaction, `LFM2-ColBERT` for multilingual retrieval
    - Links: [HF link](https://huggingface.co/LiquidAI)
